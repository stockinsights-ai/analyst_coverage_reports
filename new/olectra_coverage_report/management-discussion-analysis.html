Management Discussion and Analysis (MD&A)

Key Takeaways from Recent Management Discussions

  • Capacity Expansion: Aiming to increase production to 200 buses per month by September 2024.
  • Order Book: Secured the world's largest electric bus order of 5,150 buses from MSRTC.
  • Operational Efficiency: Transitioning to a new plant to address production bottlenecks.

Insights from Conference Calls

  • Revenue and Growth Outlook: Management expects sustained growth driven by fulfilling the existing order book.
  • Profitability Expectations: Targeting EBITDA margins between 10% and 12%.
  • Strategic Initiatives: Focus on R&D for new EV designs and localized manufacturing of chassis.
  • Capital Expenditure Plans: Ongoing investments in capacity expansion and technology upgrades.

Operational Highlights

  • Delivered 1,851 electric buses and 51 electric tippers as of FY2024 Q1.
  • Emphasis on complying with battery safety norms and other regulations.

Challenges and Opportunities

  • Challenges: Supply chain disruptions, payment delays from STUs, and competition.
  • Opportunities: Government policies promoting EVs, growing market demand, and technological advancements.
