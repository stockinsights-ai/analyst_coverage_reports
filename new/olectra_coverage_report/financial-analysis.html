Detailed Financial Analysis

A. Historical Financial Performance

Revenue Analysis

Sales grew from ₹92.15 crore in FY2015 to ₹1,090.76 crore in FY2023, a CAGR of approximately 36.2%. Significant growth due to increased demand for electric buses.

Profitability Analysis

  • Gross Profit Margin: Declined from 36.9% in FY2015 to 24.9% in FY2023.
  • EBITDA Margin: Improved from 2.5% in FY2020 to 12.9% in FY2023.
  • Net Profit Margin: Reached 6.01% in FY2023 after recovering from a net loss in FY2019.

Cost Structure Analysis

  • Raw Material Costs: Increased due to higher production but managed as a percentage of sales.
  • Employee Costs: Grew in line with business expansion.
  • Other Operating Expenses: Controlled to improve operational efficiency.

Expense Management

Implemented cost-saving initiatives and optimized procurement and supply chain processes.

Working Capital Management

  • Receivables: Increased to ₹630.48 crore in FY2023, indicating extended credit terms.
  • Inventory: Rose to ₹141.99 crore in FY2023, reflecting preparation for higher production.

Need for improved cash collection and inventory turnover.

Cash Flow Analysis

  • Operating Cash Flow: Negative ₹10.27 crore in FY2023.
  • Investing Activities: Continued capital expenditure for capacity expansion.
  • Financing Activities: Raised funds through debt and equity to support growth.

Balance Sheet Strength

  • Asset Composition: Increase in fixed assets due to new plant and equipment.
  • Debt Levels: Borrowings at ₹133.7 crore in FY2023.
  • Debt-to-Equity Ratio: Low at 0.16, indicating a strong equity base.

Return Ratios

  • Return on Equity (ROE): Improved to 8.11% in FY2023.
  • Return on Assets (ROA): Reflects efficient asset utilization.

B. Segment-wise Financial Performance

  • EV Segment: Contributed 87.07% to turnover in FY2023.
  • Power Insulators: Stable performance with growing exports.
  • Electric Trucks: Emerging contributions with potential for growth.

D. Peer Comparison

Olectra leads in the electric bus segment but faces competition from established players like Tata Motors. Financial metrics indicate stronger growth rates but lower margins compared to some competitors.

E. Financial Forecasts and Projections

Assumptions

  • Continued government support for EVs.
  • Successful expansion of production capacity.
  • Stable raw material prices.

Projected Financials

  • Sales: Expected to grow by 15% annually over the next three years.
  • EBITDA Margin: Anticipated to improve to 14% by FY2026.
  • Net Profit Margin: Projected to reach 7% by FY2026.
