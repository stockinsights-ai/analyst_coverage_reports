Executive Summary

Investment Thesis

Olectra Greentech Ltd., a pioneer in India's electric vehicle industry, is strategically positioned to capitalize on the rapid growth of the electric bus market. With a robust order book, strong government support, and strategic partnerships, the company is poised for significant revenue and profitability growth. However, potential risks related to execution, competition, and financial management should be considered.

Key Investment Highlights

Market Leadership in Electric Buses

  • Net order book of 10,818 electric buses.
  • Contracts with state transport undertakings and private entities like Microsoft.

Strategic Partnership with BYD Auto

  • Extended cooperation agreement until 2030.
  • Enhances technological capabilities and product development.

Strong Revenue Growth

  • 45% YoY increase in consolidated revenue to ₹313.9 crore in Q1 FY'25.
  • Management expects to double deliveries in the next quarter.

Aggressive Capacity Expansion

  • Plans to ramp up production to 5,000 buses/month by year-end.
  • Targeting over 10,000 buses/month in FY'26.

Government Support and Industry Tailwinds

  • Benefits from policies like FAME II promoting EV adoption.
  • Environmental concerns drive demand for electric transportation.
