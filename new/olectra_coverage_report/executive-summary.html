Executive Summary

Investment Thesis

Olectra Greentech Ltd. (Olectra) is a leading player in India's electric vehicle (EV) industry, primarily focusing on electric buses. With strong government support for electrification, a robust order book, strategic partnerships, and consistent revenue growth, Olectra is well-positioned to capitalize on the burgeoning EV market. The company's focus on innovation, capacity expansion, and operational efficiency enhances its growth prospects.

Key Investment Highlights

  • Market Leader in Electric Buses: Olectra holds a significant market share in India's electric bus segment, with a cumulative delivery of 1,851 electric buses and an outstanding order book of 10,818 buses as of FY2024.
  • Strategic Partnership with BYD: Collaboration with BYD since 2015 has enabled technology transfer and product development, strengthening Olectra's competitive edge.
  • Robust Financial Growth: The company has exhibited a Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) of approximately 36.2% in sales from FY2015 to FY2023.
  • Government Support: Favorable policies aiming to transition 800,000 buses to electric by 2030 provide a conducive environment for growth.

Valuation and Recommendation

Considering the company's strong fundamentals, market position, and growth potential, we initiate coverage on Olectra Greentech Ltd. with a "Buy" recommendation. Our valuation, based on a blend of Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) and relative valuation methods, yields a target price of ₹2,150, implying an upside potential of approximately 23% from the current market price of ₹1,745.85.
