Investment Risks

Operational Risks

  • Supply Chain Disruptions: Dependence on battery suppliers could affect production.
  • Capacity Constraints: Delays in expanding production facilities may hinder growth.

Financial Risks

  • Working Capital Management: High receivables could impact liquidity.
  • Cash Flow Volatility: Negative operating cash flows may strain finances.

Market Risks

  • Competition: Increased competition may pressure margins.
  • Demand Fluctuations: Changes in government policies or economic conditions could affect demand.

Regulatory Risks

  • Policy Changes: Alterations in subsidies or regulations could impact profitability.
  • Compliance Costs: Adapting to new safety norms may require additional investments.

Other Company-Specific Risks

  • Technological Obsolescence: Rapid advancements may render current offerings outdated.
  • Dependence on Key Partnerships: Over-reliance on partners like BYD poses risks if collaborations change.
