Valuation and Recommendation

Valuation Methodologies Used

  • Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) Analysis
  • Relative Valuation Metrics

Assumptions in Valuation Models

  • Revenue Growth Rate: 15% annually for the next three years.
  • EBITDA Margin Expansion: Improvement to 14% by FY2026.
  • Discount Rate (WACC): Assumed at 12%.
  • Terminal Growth Rate: Assumed at 3%.

Price Target Calculation

DCF Valuation: Indicates a fair value of ₹2,200 per share.
Relative Valuation: Based on an industry average EV/EBITDA multiple of 15x, suggests a value of ₹2,100 per share.
Weighted Average Target Price: ₹2,150.

Sensitivity Analysis

  • Revenue Growth Variations: A 2% change in growth rate impacts the valuation by approximately ₹100 per share.
  • Discount Rate Changes: A 1% increase in WACC decreases valuation by around ₹80 per share.

Investment Recommendation

Recommendation: Buy
Justification: Strong market position, robust order book, supportive industry trends, and reasonable valuation support the investment case. Risks are present but manageable with the company's strategies.
