Company Overview: Mahindra & Mahindra Ltd

1. History & Evolution

Founded in 1945 as a steel trading company, Mahindra & Mahindra Ltd (M&M) quickly diversified into the manufacturing of tractors and automotive vehicles. Key milestones include the joint venture with International Harvester in 1961, and its public listing on the Bombay Stock Exchange in 1956. Over the decades, M&M expanded into various sectors including finance, technology, and agribusiness.

2. Corporate Structure

  • Parent Group: Mahindra Group
  • Subsidiaries: Mahindra Finance, Tech Mahindra, Mahindra Electric, and more.
  • Key Sectors: Automotive, Farm Equipment, Financial Services, IT Services, and Real Estate.

3. Overall Business Model

M&M operates a diversified business model, centered on manufacturing and services. The company generates revenue from automotive manufacturing, agribusiness (tractors and farm machinery), financial services, and technology services. Their strategy focuses on leveraging synergies across sectors to provide sustainable value to customers and stakeholders.
