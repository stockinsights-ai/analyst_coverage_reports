Company Overview: Larsen & Toubro Limited (NSE: LT)

1. History & Evolution

Founded in 1938 by Danish engineers Henning Holck-Larsen and Soren Kristian Toubro, L&T has grown into a global conglomerate. Initially focused on engineering and construction, it expanded into technology, manufacturing, and digital services.

2. Corporate Structure

  • Construction & Infrastructure: L&T Construction undertakes large-scale projects in India and abroad.
  • Heavy Engineering: Specializes in manufacturing critical components for energy and defense sectors.
  • Information Technology Services: Through LTIMindtree and L&T Technology Services.
  • Financial Services: Managed through L&T Finance Holdings.

3. Overall Business Model

L&T operates on a diversified business model, offering engineering, construction, IT services, and financial products. It emphasizes project management and technological innovation, sustaining itself through long-term contracts and diversified revenue streams.

4. Revenue Streams

Segment Revenue Contribution
Engineering, Procurement, Construction (EPC) Major contributor through large-scale projects.
IT Services (LTIMindtree, LTTS) Significant income from digital services and R&D.
Financial Services (L&T Finance Holdings) Key contributor through retail finance and asset management.

5. Value Proposition

L&T offers cutting-edge engineering solutions across diverse sectors. Its value lies in delivering large-scale projects efficiently and leveraging advanced technologies for sustainable growth. The company's leadership in technology and infrastructure supports India's growth, while also expanding globally.

6. Management Team

  • S. N. Subrahmanyan: Chairman & Managing Director
  • R. Shankar Raman: Chief Financial Officer
  • Subramanian Sarma: Whole-time Director, Energy
  • Other executives manage various divisions, contributing to the company's operational excellence.

7. Profiles of Key Executives

S. N. Subrahmanyan has over three decades of experience at L&T and has led the company to expand its footprint globally. R. Shankar Raman is known for driving financial prudence, while Subramanian Sarma is instrumental in L&T's growth in the energy sector.

8. Key Management Changes

The company has seen a stable leadership team with no major recent changes, ensuring continuity in its strategic initiatives.

9. Recent Strategic Initiatives

  • Sustainability: Commitment to renewable energy projects.
  • Technology Advancements: Investment in AI, IoT, and digital R&D.
  • Global Expansion: Focus on growth in Middle Eastern and Southeast Asian markets.
