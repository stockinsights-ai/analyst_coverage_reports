Result Update Report for FY25 Q1 - Larsen & Toubro Limited

1. About

Larsen & Toubro Limited (L&T) is a leading Indian multinational conglomerate engaged in technology, engineering, construction, manufacturing, and financial services. With operations in over 30 countries, L&T is renowned for its execution capabilities in large and complex projects across various sectors, solidifying its position as a premier EPC contractor globally.

2. Business

  • Engineering & Construction Excellence: L&T specializes in executing large-scale infrastructure projects, including transportation, power, water, and urban development.
  • Diversified Portfolio: The company operates across multiple segments such as Infrastructure, Energy, Hydrocarbon, IT & Technology Services, Financial Services, and Manufacturing.
  • Global Footprint: Significant international presence, particularly in the Middle East, Africa, and Southeast Asia, contributing to diversified revenue streams.
  • Technological Innovation: Investments in digital technologies, AI, IoT, and automation to enhance project execution and operational efficiency.
  • Robust Order Book: Strong order book of ₹4.91 trillion as of June 2024, providing revenue visibility for the coming years.

3. Why We Like Larsen & Toubro

  • Consistent Financial Performance: Demonstrated robust revenue and profit growth over the years, showcasing financial stability.
  • Leadership Position: Market leader in EPC projects with competitive advantages and economies of scale.
  • Strong Order Pipeline: Healthy prospects pipeline of ₹9.07 trillion for the remaining nine months of FY25.
  • Focus on High-Growth Sectors: Strategic investments in renewable energy, digital services, and infrastructure align with global trends.
  • Improving Return Ratios: Enhanced ROE and ROCE indicate efficient capital utilization and profitability.

4. Recent Updates

  • Strong Q1 FY25 Performance: Revenue up by 15% YoY to ₹55,119.82 crore; PAT increased by 12% to ₹2,785.72 crore.
  • Credit Rating Upgrades: Received 'BBB+' rating with a stable outlook from S&P and Fitch, reflecting strong financial health.
  • ESG Rating Improvement: MSCI ESG rating upgraded from 'BB' to 'BBB', highlighting advancements in environmental and safety standards.
  • Strategic Divestments: Completed the sale of stake in L&T Infrastructure Development Projects Limited, enhancing ROE.
  • Major Order Wins: Secured significant orders in renewable energy and offshore sectors, strengthening market position.
